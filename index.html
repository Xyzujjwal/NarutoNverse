Naruto Uzumaki

Clan: Uzumaki

Abilities: Shadow Clone Jutsu, Rasengan, Sage Mode, Kurama Chakra Mode

Summary: Naruto Uzumaki is a shinobi of Konohagakure. He became the jinchūriki of the Nine-Tails on the day of his birth, which caused him to be ostracized by the village. Despite this, he worked hard to gain recognition and later became the Seventh Hokage.

Sasuke Uchiha

Clan: Uchiha

Abilities: Sharingan, Rinnegan, Chidori, Amaterasu

Summary: Sasuke Uchiha is one of the last surviving members of the Uchiha clan. He seeks revenge against his brother Itachi and later aims to reform the shinobi world in his own way.

Gaara

Clan: Kazekage

Abilities: Sand Manipulation, Shukaku Jinchūriki, Shield of Sand

Summary: Gaara is the Fifth Kazekage of Sunagakure. Originally a cold-hearted killer due to his harsh upbringing, he later becomes an important ally to Naruto and works to protect his village.

Obito Uchiha

Clan: Uchiha

Abilities: Sharingan, Kamui, Wood Release, Six Paths Power

Summary: Obito Uchiha was a member of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan. He was believed to have died during the Third Shinobi World War but was saved by Madara Uchiha. Disillusioned by the world, he later took on the identity of "Tobi" and manipulated events to start the Fourth Shinobi World War. However, he eventually redeemed himself and sacrificed his life to protect the world.

Minato Namikaze

Clan: Namikaze

Abilities: Flying Thunder God, Rasengan, Sage Mode, Sealing Techniques

Summary: Minato Namikaze, also known as the "Yellow Flash of the Leaf," was the Fourth Hokage of Konoha. He was a prodigy renowned for his incredible speed, intelligence, and mastery of the Flying Thunder God Technique. Minato sealed the Nine-Tails inside his newborn son, Naruto, sacrificing his life to protect the village. His strategic thinking, teleportation abilities, and raw combat power made him one of the strongest Hokage in history.
